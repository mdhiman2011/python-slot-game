This program is the dummy bet winning slot machine. Here are the steps involved to run the game:
1.) Player has to first enter the total deposited amount to Player
2.) Player starts the game
3.) Below inpute are need from player
    a.) Please enter a symbol between P, Q and R for bet R.
    b.) Enter the number of line to bet on ranges between 1 - 5
    c.) What amount you want to bet on each line? $
4.) Message is diplayed for the selection in nice way
5.) Player sees the rnadomly generated matrix and can find the occurence of selected character.
6.) Message will be displayed of either Win or loose.
7.) Player can again choose to continue play or quit.
8.) Balance amount is displayed as per the the result(Win or Loose).
